##  Research

I am currently working at the cosmic axion spin precession experiment (**CASPEr**) in the [Budker Group](https://budker.uni-mainz.de/). We want to use nuclear magnetic resonance (**NMR**) and **quantum sensing** (superconducting quantum interference device, or SQUIDðŸ¦‘) to find **axion** or **axionlike particles**, which are **dark matter** candidates.
