Over the years, Prof. Dmitry Budker, Prof. Derek Kimball, Dr. Andreas Trabesinger, and I worked out a few notes on the training of scientific presentations. For the convenience of my Chinese friends, I collected these notes and translated them to Chinese, as *Some Rules for Good Scientific Presentations* (科研报告的规矩方圆) and uploaded it to [Zenodo](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18644045).
